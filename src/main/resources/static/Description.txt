I’m a cheeky indie-pop artist from Canterbury, chock-filled with banter and warm nostalgic sounds. My first record was 'SUBURBAN BALLET', a dream-pop record centred around British nightlife. These images are much of what makes up my lyrics, which is supported by loops of guitar, keyboard and drums - all created by me. I have also released 'SAINSBURYS GIRL' supporting my second EP in June 2019, which cemented his eclectic and intimate DIY music video style. Someone’s called my music 'odd but beautifully manifesting artist', which was very nice – but then again I also got a review saying ‘if your music was a fruit, it would be an orange’.  … - dan
